Mayank Gupta

Research Scientist / Data Strategist

Karlsruhe, Germany

Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)

Mayank Gupta is a research scientist at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, where he develops data-driven strategies that bring together digital twins, sensor networks, and resilience planning for critical civil infrastructure.

His work blends physics-based simulation, machine learning, and open data practices to detect emerging risks, support maintenance prioritization, and translate insights from laboratories to real-world assets.

Research Focus

  • Climate-adaptive digital twins that steer resilient decisions for transportation and energy corridors.
  • Physics-aware machine learning models that retain interpretability under sparse instrumentation.
  • Community-centered sensing platforms and open-source tools that invite collaboration across engineering teams.
