Research Interests

My research explores conservation genetics of marine life with an applied focus on aquaculture and sustainable fishing in the U.S. This work blends population genomics, digital twins, and machine learning to detect emerging threats and translate lab-scale insights to real-world assets.

I aim to build interpretable, physics-aware models that remain robust under sparse data while empowering practitioners and communities through open-source sensing platforms and transparent dashboards.

Research Interests

Selected projects bringing experimental evidence and computational foresight together to keep infrastructure systems adaptive and transparent.

Hydration and Microstructure development

Exploring how nanoscale hydration reactions steer paste rheology, we combine diffraction, calorimetry, and in-situ imaging to map evolving pore structure under varied curing regimes. These insights guide additive selection and scheduling to keep reinforced systems resilient during early-age loading demands.

Reaction transport for Durability

Investigating coupled reaction-transport phenomena to predict long-term durability, I model how ingress-driven chemistry shifts pore connectivity while aligning experiments on ionic diffusion and carbonation to benchmark transport coefficients for accelerated service-life assessments. This supports maintenance strategies and resilient material selection.

Poromechanical modeling

Coupling fluid-solid interaction physics with experimental data, I develop poromechanical models that capture stress-driven fluid redistribution and its impact on durability. These simulations guide instrumentation placement and validate predictive maintenance scenarios for saturated infrastructure assets.

Multiscale modeling of concrete

Using linked microscopic, mesoscopic, and continuum simulations, I bridge chemistry, pore structure, and structural response to predict cracking, creep, and durability at service life scales. These models inform mix design and sensor placement to safeguard aging concrete systems under complex loading.
